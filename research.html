5 Reasons you are your own Worst Enemy

  1. You assume.

    We subconsciously fill in the blanks with answers that are incorrect. Assuming can lead to many misunderstood situations and feelings.

  2. Self Doubt.

    Doubting yourself only limits your ability to progress and learn from past mistakes.

  3. Harsh Self Critic

    Oftentimes, people take self-judgment too far to the point of never being satisfied with yourself.

  4. Too goal oriented

    Focusing solely on goals makes you take everyday life for granted.

  5. You Over-Analyze

    Over thinking everything will make you live in your head rather than the real world.
