Your Own Worst Enemy

Mental health is an issue that is not priotized enough in our society. How you treat yourself has a huge affect on your physical well being. A person's mentality also reflects on how they treat others around them . Often as humans, our biggest limitation is ourselves. Imagine how much you could accomplish if you were not in your own way. My research page discusses reasons why you are your own worst enemy.
